Frequently Asked Questions

Growth monitoring helps track a child’s physical and developmental progress, enabling early detection of nutritional, hormonal, or developmental concerns for timely intervention.

Regular checkups are recommended during infancy (monthly initially), followed by periodic visits every few months during early childhood to ensure healthy development.

It includes height and weight tracking, BMI evaluation, developmental milestone assessment, nutrition review, and personalized health guidance for optimal growth.
